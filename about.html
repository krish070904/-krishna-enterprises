About Krishna Enterprises

Krishna Enterprises specializes in manufacturing machine spare parts for large-scale industries such as Eaton, Grind Master, and Glasco. With a focus on quality and precision, we deliver solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

Our Expertise

We excel in providing customized solutions based on AutoCAD drawings provided by our clients. Our manufacturing process ensures efficient production and timely delivery of high-quality spare parts.

Whether it's through courier services or direct delivery to our clients' facilities, we ensure seamless logistics to meet our clients' requirements.
